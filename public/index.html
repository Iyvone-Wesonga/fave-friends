Coming up with Fave

Meet Miriam Kegeshi

I love expressing myself through various forms of art. This made me develop a growing interest in anime, which in my opinion, is art expressed. I enjoy the unique storylines and imaginative worlds that are presented in anime. The creativity and attention to detail in the animation, you could say, enthralls me. Also, music is a passion of mine. I love to sing and play the piano, with hopes of expanding my musical genius (｡•̀ᴗ-)✧.
I find joy in the process of creating music and the emotions it can evoke in myself and others. Music is expression and as you can tell by now, I am a fan of such especially considering it allows me to connect with people on a deeper level. My love for life and happiness is what motivates me to pursue my artistic endeavors. I believe that life is meant to be lived to the fullest and that we should never take a single moment for granted.

Why anime?
Isn't it for Nerds?

Anime, pronounced a · nuh · may , is a Japanese form of Cartoon that is created from scratch by hand. Over the years, the creation process has be digitalised and I can admit that I'm in love with the process. You can attribute this to the crazy and creative plots I have interacted with over some time of watching anime. Basically, I would like to share this creative genre, which I like to consider sensational, with the world hence the creation of Fave. I'm proud to tell you there is and isn't everything nerdy about anime

Why create Fave?
Why I did it
